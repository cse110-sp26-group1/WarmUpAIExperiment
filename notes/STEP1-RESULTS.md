# Refinement Round

### Our top 5 and why we picked them:

20,10,14,17,26
Candidates 20, 10, 14, 17, 26 were our top 5. We chose these because:

- 26 had a different UI look compared to the rest with lighter colors
- 17 had a lot more features
- 14 had a live commentary feed
- 10 had a lever and generally nice animation with sound
- 20 made fun of AI the best

Overall we thought that these 5 candidates followed our rubric the best compared to the others.
They had decent animations, followed the prompt most accurately, and had extra features that poked
at AI.

### Other notes

- We saw that many of the candidates generally had the same layout and color scheme despite
  independent runs.
- AI specifically reused the white/gold and blue/purple color schemes in the UI
- Many of the candidates followed similar practices in linking the js script at the bottom
  of the HTML file
- Indentation seems to be the same across each file in most of the candidates.
